About Our College

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Institute Of Technology was established in 1990 with a vision to provide quality education in rural and urban areas. We aim to nurture innovation, discipline, and excellence in technical education.

Departments

Computer Engineering

Equipping students with knowledge in AI, software development, and data science to thrive in the digital era.

Mechanical Engineering

Fostering creativity and innovation in design, thermodynamics, and automation technologies.

Electronics and Communication

Building expertise in embedded systems, VLSI design, IoT, and communication systems.

Civil Engineering

Strengthening foundations in physics, chemistry, mathematics, and communication skills.
